Contact

TypeTest is a small, independently run project, and we read every message. Whether something's broken, you have an idea, or you just want to say hello, we'd like to hear from you.

Email us

The best way to reach us is by email:

infomatch@gmail.com

What to write about

  • Bug reports — if the test scores incorrectly or won't start, tell us what happened and which browser and device you're using.
  • Feedback — anything that would make the test clearer, fairer, or faster.
  • Suggestions — a passage, mode, or feature you'd like to see added.
  • Corrections — spotted a mistake in one of our guides or pages? Let us know and we'll fix it.

Response time

We usually reply within a few days. There's a real person reading your note — not an automated system — so a little detail goes a long way.

Privacy

The typing test runs entirely in your browser and never uploads what you type. If you email us, we only see what you choose to include in your message. See our Privacy Policy for more.
