What is a quote typing test?

A quote typing test measures the same words-per-minute (WPM) and accuracy as a standard typing test, but instead of a generic filler passage, you type a real, correctly-attributed quote — a memorable line from a novel, a classic film, or something a historical figure actually said. It is the same skill, with more interesting material to type.

How to use the quote typing test

  • Choose a duration. Pick 15, 30, or 60 seconds, just like the main typing test.
  • Start typing. Click the passage or start typing to begin. The timer starts on your first keystroke.
  • Finish the quote or run out of time. The test ends when you complete the quote or the timer hits zero, whichever comes first.
  • Want a different quote? Use the New quote button to load another one from the pool at random.

Where these quotes come from

Every quote in the pool is a well-known, widely documented line — spanning classic literature, famous film dialogue, and things historical figures actually said or wrote — shown with the correct author or source. We deliberately stick to quotes that are consistently worded across reliable references rather than paraphrasing from memory, so you are practicing on text you can trust.

Why type quotes instead of random text?

Typing familiar, well-crafted sentences can be more engaging than random filler text, and quotes often include punctuation, capitalization, and varied sentence rhythm that mirror real-world typing better than simple word lists. If you are practicing daily, rotating between the standard typing test, this quote test, and the code typing test keeps your practice varied.

Frequently asked questions

Where do the quotes come from?
Every quote in this test is a real, well-known line from literature, film, or a historical figure, shown with its correct author attribution. We only include quotes that are widely documented and consistently worded across reliable sources.
Is the quote typing test free?
Yes. The quote typing test is completely free, requires no signup or account, and runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you type is uploaded anywhere.
How is WPM calculated on the quote typing test?
WPM uses the same formula as a standard typing test: correctly typed characters divided by five (a standardized word length), scaled to a full minute. Only correctly typed characters count toward your score.
Can I practice with a specific quote length?
Quotes are chosen at random from a curated pool each time you start or restart the test. Use the New quote button to skip to a different one if you want a shorter or longer passage.
