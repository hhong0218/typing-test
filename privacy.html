Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

TypeTest ("we", "us") operates the typing speed test at typing.quietools.com. This policy explains what information is and is not collected when you use the site. We have tried to keep it short and honest, because the tool itself collects very little.

What data we collect

The typing test runs entirely in your browser. The text you type is processed on your own device to calculate your words per minute (WPM) and accuracy. Nothing you type is ever uploaded to or stored on our servers.

  • No account, no server-side storage. There is no signup, and we keep no database of users or results.
  • Local storage only. Your best WPM score is saved in your browser's local storage so it can be shown to you on return visits. This value stays on your device and is never sent to us. You can clear it any time through your browser settings.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage — for example, how many people complete a test. This may set cookies and collect standard technical information such as your browser type and approximate region. It does not identify you personally, and we do not combine it with the text you type.

Cookies and Advertising

This site uses cookies to improve your experience. We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Third-party services

The third parties referenced above (Google Analytics and Google AdSense) handle data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they process information.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at infomatch@gmail.com.
