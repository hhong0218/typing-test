About TypeTest

TypeTest is a free, no-nonsense typing speed test that tells you how fast and how accurately you type — in seconds, right in your browser.

Why we built it

Most typing tests bury a simple question — "how fast can I type?" — under accounts, popups, and slow pages. We wanted the opposite: open the page, start typing, get a clear WPM and accuracy score. No signup, no waiting, no clutter.

How the test works

You choose a duration (15, 30, or 60 seconds) and type a short English passage. As you type, each character is checked against the target text. When time runs out — or you finish the passage — we show your words per minute, your accuracy, and your personal best.

  • WPM uses the standard five-character "word", counting only correctly typed characters, so it reflects usable speed.
  • Accuracy is the share of your keystrokes that matched the target text.
  • Your best score is saved locally in your browser so you can chase it over time.

Privacy first

Everything happens on your device. The text you type is never uploaded, and we keep no account or server-side record of your results. Read the full Privacy Policy for details.
